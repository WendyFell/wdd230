Description of Target Audience

Personas

One: Small business owner who wants more exposure of business to the community and to network with other business owners. He is 35 years old, married with 2 children, has just completed a degree in business so that he can start a new restaurant. He has worked in the food industry since he was a teenager and loves to create new recipes. He uses his mobile devices almost exclusively for work: writing recipes and going on social media showing his creations. He uses a laptop at home.

Two: School administrator who wants to have an event for volunteers to come help out at the school fundraising event-a Fun Run. She is 52 years old, is unmarried, has worked in the school industry since she was 24. She has been a teacher most her career. She is comfortable with both the computer and mobile devices, she will mostly use a computer as it is easier to navigate her tasks.

Five Listed Scenarios

Scenarios that describe the visitors pupose in visiting and using the site of which the site content can provide the answers such as:

  1. What are the upcoming events in the community for the Chamber?
  2. How does someone become a member?
  3. What are some job opportunities in the community?
  4. How do I contact the chamber to schedule a volunteer opportunity?
  5. What are the businesses associated with the chamber and how can I contact them?
